This one came from an estate sale over on Lee Drive in Anniston, run by Estate Sales by Anne Reid. We went on the final day, which is usually when the crowds have thinned out and you can move through the rooms without bumping elbows.

The haul is a good mix of things. On the record side, there's a Hi Fi Record pressing of *Taboo* by Arthur Lyman and a copy of *Mantovani Film Encores Volume 1* on the Stereophonic label — the Mantovani still has some plastic wrap on it. For tableware, there's a ruffled glass bowl and a silver-tone serving dish with decorative handles. Rounding things out are a couple of old pipe wrenches showing plenty of wear and what looks like a cast-iron pipe cutter with a knob handle.

Not a bad haul for a final day. Items will be listed across our shops as we get them cleaned up and photographed — keep an eye on our storefronts to see what comes available.
