We picked this load up June 20 from an East Alabama auction, and it is very much still in the 'came off the truck' stage. The garage corner is stacked deep with framed prints, a wicker basket full of VHS tapes, crocheted throws, cardboard boxes we haven't opened yet, and a couple of wooden storage boxes tucked in among it all.

There is a small painted wood crate with a hinged lid, a carved wooden magazine rack, some glass pieces peeking out near the back, and a stack of hardback books along the shelf edge. A few plastic totes and a blue tarp bag round out the pile, along with what looks like an old bathroom scale and some smaller boxed items we have not gotten to yet.

This is very much a sorting-in-progress post. As we go through it piece by piece, clean things up, and figure out what is what, we will start listing items across our usual spots. Check back as things come online, or follow along here for updates as the pile turns into actual listings.
